Classes

Bodybuilding

Bodybuilding is a sport that involves progressive resistance exercise to build, control, and develop one’s muscles via hypertrophy.

Fitness

Fitness is a state of being physically and mentally healthy, which can be achieved through proper nutrition, regular exercise, and adequate rest1. It is a broad term that encompasses various aspects of physical activity, including strength training, cardio, and flexibility exercises.

Aerobics

It is a great way to burn calories, improve your endurance, and reduce stress. If you’re new to aerobics or exercise in general, it’s important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts.

Pilates

It is a form of exercise that focuses on building strength, flexibility, and endurance through a series of repetitive movements. It is often performed on a yoga mat or other equipment and emphasizes the use of the core muscles to promote stability and balance.

Wheight Lifting

It is a great way to improve your overall fitness, increase your metabolism, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases. With time and dedication, you’ll be able to build strength, improve your physique, and achieve your health goals!

Nutrition

Nutrition plays a crucial role in achieving your fitness goals. A balanced diet of macronutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, and fats, along with essential micronutrients, enables the body to carry out its necessary functions.
